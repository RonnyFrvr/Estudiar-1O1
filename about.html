About


About Estudiar-1O1:


Estudiar-1O1 is built to aid students in their journey throughout school by giving them essential skills to develop their study habits, improve memory, and succeed in exams. No tricks, just proven strategies.

What We Do:


We teach practical techniques that save time and boosts results. This guide covers essential study skills, note-taking strategies, as well as exam preparation skills. By the end of this you will:
  1. Learn how to properly take notes during class.
  2. Learn how to spot important information during class sessions.
  3. Feel more confident for you next exam.

Who It's For:


This guide aids high school students, college students, or even self-learners. If you want to learn how to learn, you belong here.
